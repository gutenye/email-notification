## Getting Started

1. Follow the [Quick Deploy](../../../docs/Deployment.md) guide, deploy Guten Email Notification service.

2. Use it

   ```sh
   curl https://YOUR_DOMAIN/YOUR_API_KEY?_template=Ntfy&title=Title -d 'Message'
   ```
